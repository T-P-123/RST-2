### Task 2: Add `\begin{titlepage}` block to `main.tex`

**Files:**
- Modify: `priklady/reseni_souborne/main.tex` (replace `\title{...}\author{}\date{}\maketitle\thispagestyle{fancy}` block with a `titlepage` environment)

- [ ] **Step 1: Read current main.tex preamble + title block**

Open `priklady/reseni_souborne/main.tex`. Locate two regions to change:

(A) Title commands (currently around lines 51–59):
```latex

    \vspace{-1cm}
    \textbf{Rizika a spolehlivost technických systémů}\\[0.3cm]
    \Large Souborné řešení příkladů 1E--5E\\[0.2cm]
    \large Akademický rok 2025/2026
}
\author{}

```

(B) Title invocation right after `\begin{document}`:
```latex

```

- [ ] **Step 2: Remove the `\title{...}\author{}\date{}` block**

Delete lines containing `\title{%` through the closing `}` of `\title{...}`, plus the empty `\author{}` and `\date{}` lines. Result: nothing where the title block was.

- [ ] **Step 3: Replace `\maketitle\thispagestyle{fancy}` with the titlepage environment**

In the document body, replace:

```latex
\begin{document}
\maketitle
\thispagestyle{fancy}
```

with:

```latex
\begin{document}

\begin{titlepage}
\centering

% --- Logo VUT (volitelné) ---
\IfFileExists{logo/vut.pdf}{%
    \noindent\includegraphics[width=4cm]{logo/vut.pdf}\hfill\strut
}{%
    \IfFileExists{logo/vut.png}{%
        \noindent\includegraphics[width=4cm]{logo/vut.png}\hfill\strut
    }{%
        \noindent{\bfseries\Large VUT FSI}\hfill\strut
    }%
}

\vspace{0.4cm}
\noindent\rule{\textwidth}{0.8pt}

\vspace{1.2cm}

{\large Vysoké učení technické v Brně\par}
{\large Fakulta strojního inženýrství\par}
{\large Ústav mechaniky těles, mechatroniky a biomechaniky\par}

\vfill

{\Huge\bfseries ÚLOHY E\par}
\vspace{0.6cm}
{\Large Souborné řešení příkladů 1E\,--\,5E\par}

\vspace{1.2cm}

{\Large\itshape Spolehlivost konstrukcí\par}

\vfill

{\large\bfseries Členové týmu\par}
\vspace{0.3cm}
{\large
Tomáš Pavlíček\\[2pt]
Tomáš Sláčik\\[2pt]
Vašek Horčic\\[2pt]
Eduard Paclík\par}

\vspace{1.2cm}

{\normalsize Akademický rok 2025/2026\par}

\end{titlepage}
```

The `\IfFileExists` macro is built-in LaTeX — no extra package needed.
